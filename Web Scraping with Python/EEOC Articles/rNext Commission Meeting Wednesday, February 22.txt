<h1>Next Commission Meeting Wednesday, February 22</h1>
</div>
<div align="left"><p>WASHINGTON – The U.S. Equal Employment Opportunity Commission (EEOC) will hold a meeting on Wednesday, February 22, at 9:30 a.m. (Eastern Time), at agency headquarters, 131 M Street, N.E. In accordance with the Sunshine Act, the open session of the meeting will be open to public observation of the Commission’s deliberations and voting. The remainder of the meeting will be closed. </p>
<p>The following matters are scheduled for consideration during the open session of the meeting:<br/>
</p>
<ul>
<li>Announcement of Notation Votes</li>
<li>Vote on EEOC Strategic Plan for Fiscal Years 2012-2016.</li>
</ul>
<p>For the open session, seating is limited and it is suggested that visitors arrive 30 minutes before the meeting in order to be processed through security and escorted to the meeting room.  </p>
<p>The Commission agenda is subject to revision. Any matter not discussed or concluded may be carried over to a later meeting.  Additional information about the hearing, when available, will be posted at <a href="https://www.eeoc.gov/eeoc/meetings/index.cfm" id="CP___PAGEID=12655,index.cfm,31|">http://www.eeoc.gov/eeoc/meetings/index.cfm</a>.</p>
<p>The EEOC enforces federal laws prohibiting employment discrimination.   Further information about the EEOC is available on its web site at www.eeoc.gov.<br/>
</p></div>
