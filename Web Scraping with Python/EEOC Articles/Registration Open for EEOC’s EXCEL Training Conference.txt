<h1>Registration Open for EEOC’s EXCEL Training Conference</h1>
</div>
<div align="left"><p style="text-align: center;"><strong><em>Federal Agency's 20th Annual Training Event for Public and Private Sectors Taking Place June 27-29 in Chicago</em></strong></p>
<p>WASHINGTON - The U.S. Equal Employment Opportunity Commission (EEOC) announced today that registration is now open for its 20th annual Examining Conflicts in Employment Laws (EXCEL) training conference on preventing employment discrimination for
both the public and private sectors. The conference will take place June 27-29 in Chicago.</p>
<p>EXCEL's 20th anniversary theme is <em>Embracing the Future: People, Purpose, Passion</em>.</p>
<p>EXCEL is the EEOC's premier training conference and features comprehensive training workshops and events specifically designed for federal EEO managers, supervisors and practitioners. EXCEL also features the most up-to-date guidance and
information for private-sector human resource professionals, attorneys, ADR specialists and EEO consultants.</p>
<p>Additional details about the conference, including agendas, pricing information and online registration are available at <a href="http://eeotraining.eeoc.gov/excelmain.html">http://eeotraining.eeoc.gov/excelmain.html</a></p>
<p>Registration information may also be obtained by calling 1-866-446-0940 or via e-mail: <a href="mailto:eeoc.traininginstitute@eeoc.gov" id="mailto:eeoc.traininginstitute@eeoc.gov|">eeoc.traininginstitute@eeoc.gov</a>. </p>
<p>EEOC advances opportunity in the workplace by enforcing federal laws prohibiting employment discrimination. More information is available at <a href="https://www.eeoc.gov" id="https://www.eeoc.gov|">www.eeoc.gov</a>. Stay connected with the
latest EEOC news by subscribing to our <a href="https://public.govdelivery.com/accounts/USEEOC/subscriber/new" id="https://public.govdelivery.com/accounts/USEEOC/subscriber/new|">email updates</a>.</p></div>
