<h1>Registration Now Open For Excel Training  For Public and Private Sectors</h1>
</div>
<div align="left"><p style="text-align: center;"><strong><em>Federal Agency's 19th Annual Premier Training Event to Take Place July 18-21 in San Francisco, CA</em></strong></p>
<p>WASHINGTON -- The U.S. Equal Employment Opportunity Commission (EEOC) today announced that registration is now open for its 19th annual Examining Conflicts in Employment Laws, (EXCEL) training conference on preventing employment discrimination
for both public and private sectors. The conference will take place July 18-21 in San Francisco, Calif.</p>
<p>EXCEL is EEOC's premier training conference for professionals and includes separate agendas for the federal sector and private sector, which includes state and local governments.  The comprehensive training workshops and events are designed
for EEO managers, supervisors, practitioners, HR professionals, attorneys, ADR specialists and other interested parties.  </p>
<p>EXCEL's theme this year is <em>Inform, Invest and Innovate</em>.</p>
<p>Additional details about the conference, including agendas, pricing information and online registration are available at <a href="http://eeotraining.eeoc.gov/excelmain.html" id="http://eeotraining.eeoc.gov/excelmain.html|">http://eeotraining.eeoc.gov/excelmain.html</a></p>
<p>Registration information may also be obtained by calling 1-866-446-0940 or via e-mail: <a href="mailto:eeoc.traininginstitute@eeoc.gov" id="mailto:eeoc.traininginstitute@eeoc.gov|">eeoc.traininginstitute@eeoc.gov</a>. </p>
<p>EEOC is responsible for enforcing laws prohibiting employment discrimination in the public and private sectors. Additional information about the agency can be found at <a href="https://www.eeoc.gov" id="https://www.eeoc.gov|">www.eeoc.gov</a>. Follow the EXCEL Conference on Twitter at @EEOC_EXCEL.</p></div>
