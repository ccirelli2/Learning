<h1>EEOC Announces "Chair's Message" Online</h1>
</div>
<div align="left"><p style="text-align: center"><strong><em>Chair Jenny Yang Pens First in Series of Articles on the Work of the Agency</em></strong></p>
<p>WASHINGTON - The U.S. Equal Employment Opportunity Commission (EEOC) today announced the first "Chair's Message" on <a href="http://www.eeoc.gov/">www.eeoc.gov</a> devised by Chair Jenny Yang to provide the public with information about the work
of the EEOC, including best practices and insights on how to prevent, stop and remedy workplace discrimination, among other information. Today's <a href="http://www.eeoc.gov/eeoc/chair/investing_in_solutions.cfm">post</a> is the first in a regular
series by Chair Yang.</p>
<p>The EEOC enforces federal laws prohibiting employment discrimination. Further information about the EEOC is available at <a href="http://www.eeoc.gov/">www.eeoc.gov</a>.</p></div>
